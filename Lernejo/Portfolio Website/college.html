Colleges

There are several colleges I am interested in.

The first college I am interested in attending is the Technical University of Dortmund, as it offers courses I am interested in, is tuition free even for foreigners due to being in Germany, and because it has a nice monorail system.

The second college I am looking at is the Technical University of Munich, since it also offers courses I am interested in, and is free due to being in Germany.

For the same reason, I am also interested in the similerly named Technical University of Berlin; it offers all of the courses I am interested as well, and has the tuition-free aspect too, since it is also in Germany.

Price for 4 years of 8 semesters: Free. If it wasn't free, I wouldn't be able to afford it and therefore would not be able to go.

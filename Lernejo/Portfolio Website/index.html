For this reason, I do lots of my own research, and have currently learned quite a lot from this method. Most of what I have learned relates to computers, although recently, I have also researched many more things, such as linguistics, history, and many things, such as supply chains, philosophy, and politics, relating to my big project, the establishment of a gift economy through the creation of Anarkikomunismolando. Anarkikomunismolando is an attempt at creating an area where people are free of property tax and automating it as much as possible so that everything can be given away for free! It also aims to provide a place for those who hate intellectual property laws, and aims to provide great political freedom, through direct democracy and the ability of one to easily leave and create another autonomous community. At the moment, I am getting better at organization, and aim to direct as many other projects as I can to helping the creation of Anarkikomunismolando.
